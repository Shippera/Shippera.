Who We Are

Shippera is a logistics and import service based in Erbil, Kurdistan, helping individuals and businesses access high-quality products from the USA and China.

Unlike other businesses, we focus on sourcing **your specific needs**, including hard-to-find car parts and advanced medical devices. Our custom import services ensure that what you request is what you get — reliably and on time.

We also import a wide range of products such as electronics, industrial machinery, and consumer goods. Our goal is to provide dependable and affordable shipping solutions tailored to the Kurdistan and Iraq region.

At Shippera, we believe in service, trust, and building long-term relationships with our customers. We’re not just moving products — we’re connecting you to the world.
